Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Quantly mobile application ("App") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data. This policy has been prepared in compliance with the Turkish Personal Data Protection Law No. 6698 ("KVKK") and the General Data Protection Regulation ("GDPR").

1. Data Controller

Application Name: Quantly
Contact: support@quantlyapp.com

2. Personal Data We Collect

Our application collects the following personal data:

a) Identity and Contact Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)

b) Financial Data

  • Expense records (amount, category, date, description)
  • Budget information
  • Payment method information (name/label only, card numbers are not stored)
  • Recurring payment information

c) Visual Data

  • Receipt/invoice images (via camera or gallery)

d) Technical Data

  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • Notification tokens (for push notifications)
  • App usage data

e) Authentication Data

  • Email and password (stored encrypted)
  • Google account information (when using Google Sign-In)
  • Apple account information (when using Apple Sign-In)

3. Purposes of Data Processing

Your personal data is processed for the following purposes:

  • Creating and managing your user account
  • Providing expense tracking and budget management services
  • Receipt scanning and automatic data extraction
  • Sending notifications (reminders, budget alerts)
  • Improving application performance
  • Fulfilling legal obligations

4. Legal Basis for Data Processing

Your personal data is processed based on the following legal grounds under Article 5 of KVKK:

  • Explicit consent: By providing your consent during registration
  • Performance of contract: Processing data necessary to provide our services to you
  • Legitimate interest: Application security and performance improvements
  • Legal obligation: Retention and reporting as required by law

5. Data Transfers

Your personal data may be transferred to the following parties:

  • Supabase (European Union): Database and authentication service provider. Data is hosted in the EU region.
  • Google LLC: Authentication data when you sign in with Google
  • Apple Inc.: Authentication data when you sign in with Apple

International data transfers are carried out in compliance with Article 9 of KVKK, either to countries with adequate protection or based on your explicit consent.

6. Data Retention Period

  • Your personal data is retained as long as your account is active.
  • When you delete your account, your identity and contact information is permanently deleted.
  • Expense and budget data is anonymized (cannot be associated with your personal information).
  • Some data may be retained for certain periods as required by legal obligations.

7. Your Rights as a Data Subject

Under Article 11 of KVKK, you have the following rights:

  1. To learn whether your personal data is being processed
  2. To request information about your processed personal data
  3. To learn the purpose of processing and whether data is used accordingly
  4. To know the third parties to whom personal data is transferred domestically or abroad
  5. To request correction of incomplete or inaccurate personal data
  6. To request deletion or destruction of personal data under the conditions set forth in KVKK
  7. To request notification of correction and deletion to third parties to whom data has been transferred
  8. To object to any result that is against you arising from the analysis of processed data exclusively through automated systems

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@quantlyapp.com. Your request will be responded to within 30 days.

8. Account Deletion and Data Anonymization

You can delete your account from within the app via Settings > Delete My Account. Account deletion is irreversible. Upon deletion:

  • Your personal information (name, email, phone) is permanently deleted.
  • Expense and budget data is anonymized.
  • Receipt images are deleted.

9. Security Measures

  • Your data is transmitted using SSL/TLS encryption.
  • Passwords are stored using secure hashing algorithms.
  • Sensitive data is stored encrypted on-device (SecureStore).
  • Database access is protected by Row Level Security (RLS) policies.

10. Cookies

Quantly is a mobile application and does not use cookies.

11. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through in-app notifications. Your continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

For questions about our privacy policy or your personal data, please contact us:

Email: support@quantlyapp.com
